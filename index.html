Privacy Policy

We may collect the following information from users:

This data is collected via in-app forms when signing in or using location-based features.

2. How We Use Your Data

3. Data Security

We use secure connections and follow industry practices to protect your data stored in Firebase. However, no system is 100% secure.

4. Data Deletion

Users can request to delete their data by contacting us at tabeebpak.1@gmail.com or through an in-app feature (if available).

5. Children's Privacy

Tabeeb is open to all users. However, we do not knowingly collect data from children under 13 without parental consent.
